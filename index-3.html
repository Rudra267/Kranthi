About Us

About KranthiSolar

Kranthi Energy Systems is a leading provider of innovative renewable energy solutions. With a strong focus on solar technology, we aim to help individuals, businesses, and industries make the transition to clean, sustainable, and cost-effective energy. Our mission is to reduce dependency on non-renewable energy sources, lower energy costs, and contribute to a greener planet by offering cutting-edge solar products.

Founded with a vision to empower people through renewable energy, Kranthi Energy Systems offers a diverse range of solar solutions, including Solar Water Heaters, Solar Security Fencing, Solar On-Grid Systems, and Solar Lighting. Our solutions are designed with the latest technologies, ensuring maximum efficiency, durability, and sustainability.

Our Products

Eco-friendly solar water heaters, solar-powered security fencing, on-grid solar systems for energy savings, and durable solar lighting solutions.

Solar Water Heaters

Harness the sun’s energy to heat water in an efficient, eco-friendly way. Our solar water heaters are designed for homes and businesses, providing reliable hot water with minimal energy consumption.

Solar Security Fencing

Enhance the security of your property with our solar-powered security fencing systems. Powered by solar energy, these fences provide reliable perimeter protection while reducing electricity costs.

Solar On-Grid Systems

Our on-grid solar systems are designed to integrate with your existing electricity grid, allowing you to generate your own clean energy while still having access to the grid. With excess energy being fed back into the grid.

Solar Lighting

From streetlights to garden lights, our solar lighting solutions provide illumination powered entirely by renewable solar energy. They are durable, efficient, and perfect for outdoor environments.
