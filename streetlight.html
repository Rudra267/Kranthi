Integrated Solar Street Lights

Integrated solar street lights are compact, all-in-one units combining a solar panel, battery, charge controller, and LED light in a single fixture. They are easy to install and ideal for small-scale applications like residential streets, parks, and gardens. These lights are an eco-friendly, reliable, and cost-effective solution, especially in areas without access to the electrical grid.

Provided By Us

Design

All-in-one integrating solar panel, battery, controller, and LED.

Power Range

10W to 80W (depending on application).

Battery Type

Lithium-ion or Lead-acid.

Battery Capacity

12V, 20Ah to 50Ah (depending on wattage).

Solar Panel

Typically 18V to 24V, 10W to 80W.

Light Source

High-efficiency LEDs (e.g., 140-170 lumens per watt).

Operation Time

8-12 hours on full charge.

Applications

Residential streets, parks, gardens, walkways, and small roads.

Why Solar Street Lights Are the Future:

Making the switch to solar-powered street lighting is a smart move for both your wallet and the planet. Here’s why:

Energy Independence:
Solar street lights use sunlight to power themselves, so you don’t need to worry about ongoing electricity costs or grid dependence.
Eco-Friendly & Sustainable:
Solar energy is clean and renewable, meaning these lights help reduce pollution and carbon emissions, making them a fantastic eco-friendly alternative to traditional lighting.
Low Maintenance Costs:
Thanks to their durable design and simple setup, these solar lights require very little upkeep, saving you money in the long run.
Automatic, Hassle-Free Operation:
With an intelligent control system, these lights turn on at dusk and off at dawn without any manual effort, making them incredibly convenient.
